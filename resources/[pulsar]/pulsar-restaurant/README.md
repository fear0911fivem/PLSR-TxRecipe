## Overview

Restaurant and food service job system for Pulsar Framework. Allows players to run food establishments — taking orders, preparing food items, and serving customers with configurable menus and job roles.

---

## Dependencies

- `pulsar-core` — framework core
- `pulsar-jobs` — restaurant job definition
- `ox_inventory` — food item handling
