## Overview

Business and entertainment system for Pulsar Framework. Covers a wide range of player-owned and scripted venues — bars, cafes, pawn shops, jewellers, bowling, boxing, real estate, and more. Each business has its own logic, duty zones, and contactless payment integration.

---

## Dependencies

- `pulsar-core` — framework core
- `pulsar-characters` — character job and ownership data
- `ox_inventory` — item handling and shop transactions
- `pulsar-pedinteraction` — ped-based shop interactions
- `oxmysql` — business data persistence
